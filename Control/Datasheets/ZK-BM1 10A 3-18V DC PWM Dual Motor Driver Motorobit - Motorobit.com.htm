ZK-BM1 10A 3-18V DC PWM Dual Motor Driver

ZK-BM1 10A 3-18V DC PWM Dual Motor Driver. 3-18VDC can control 2 motors independently. Single channel operating current is 10 amps. Since the current is above 8 amperes, it is recommended to increase the heat dissipation. The motor driver can move the motor forward and backward and adjust its speed.

Things to Consider During Use

    • When changing the forward-backward direction of the motor, be sure to stop the motor and change direction
    • Make connections carefully, there is no reverse polarity protection
    • PWM control frequency should not exceed 2kHz. Otherwise, the module will be damaged
    • If the Operating Current exceeds 8 amperes, heat dissipation equipment must be added

Specifications:

      • Supply Voltage: 3-18V DC
      • Signal Voltage: 3-18V
      • Operating Current: 10 A for each channel
      • Supported PWM Frequency Range: 0 - 2 kHz
      • Operating Temperature Range: -25 ~ 85°C
      • Module Size: 49x39x14mm
